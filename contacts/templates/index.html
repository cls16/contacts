Home

Welcome to the Contact Adder™! If you have questions about the app or don't understand something check out the two sections below on using the 'add contact' and 'contacts' functionality.

Add Contact

Simply fill out the form with the information you want your contact to have. Then press the submit button. If you get any errors, read the error message and carefully review your input. Once you submit the form without any errors, you should be redirected to the contacts page.

Contacts

If you don't have any contacts entered, then the page will display a 'no contacts to show' message. Add a contact if you want to see one. If you do have any contacts entered, they will display and there will be a checkbox next to each one. This is for the delete functionality. Simply check the box of each contact you want to delete then press the delete button at the bottom of the page.
